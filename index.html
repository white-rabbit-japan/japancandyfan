Why Us

Based in Tokyo, White Rabbit Japan is an international team working to make Japanese language, products and culture easily accessible worldwide.

Since 2004 we've:

  • Published a series of top-selling language products for Japanese learners,
  • Built an online shop with hundreds of Japanese language titles,
  • Started proxy-buying and package forwarding services for products from Japan,
  • Produced a series of audio guided tours for Tokyo neighborhoods.

White Rabbit Press Kana and Kanji Flashcards are the top-selling product of their kind in bookstores across Japan, on Amazon.com and in markets around the world.

White Rabbit Express has shipped more than 500,000 items to over 40,000 customers in 95+ countries. Working with thousands of vendors, our services are invaluable in helping Japanese companies reach the international market.
